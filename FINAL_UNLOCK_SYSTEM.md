**Requirement**: Score $50,000+ in any single game

**How to Achieve**:
- Play any game against any opponent
- Finish with a score of $50,000 or more
- Win or lose doesn't matter - just the score!

**Strategy**:
- Target high-value clues ($800, $1000, $1600, $2000)
- Bet maximum on Daily Doubles when confident
- Nail Final Jeopardy with big wager
- Play against easier opponents for better accuracy
- Recommended: Brad Rutter or Ken (high difficulty but fair)

**Why $50,000?**
- James's average winning score was ~$76,944
- $50,000 is a challenging but achievable target
- Represents mastery of high-value play

**Requirement**: Beat James Holzhauer with $30,000+

**Prerequisites**:

- Must finish with $30K+ score

**Strategy**:
- Very challenging - James is difficulty 14
- High accuracy required
- Aggressive Daily Double betting
- Need strong Final Jeopardy performance
